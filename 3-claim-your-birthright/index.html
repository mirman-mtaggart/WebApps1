The End of Speed

With the end of the Cold War came the end of the speed race. Range and maneuverability became more important than raw power. Although the Raptor is still a quick jet, its real advantages, along with its contemporaries, is its unbelievable maneuverability and weapons technology.

It's doubtful we'll ever see another air marvel like the Blackbird, still the fastest jet in history.
